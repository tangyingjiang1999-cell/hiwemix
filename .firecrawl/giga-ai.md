## Agent Canvas

AI turns every conversation into clarity. It surfaces patterns, uncovers root causes, and quantifies impact, then recommends actions that drive KPIs and revenue.

[Explore Agent Canvas](https://giga.ai/agent-canvas)

- ### Create the agent





Ground agents in your brand standards, compliance rules, and workflows so every interaction is consistent and on-policy.

- ### Define policies





Set clear guardrails in plain language. Decide what should be automated, when to escalate, and how to manage sensitive cases.

- ### Design the logic





Map conversation flows and escalation paths. Connect tools and systems so each response is grounded in business context.

- ### Test and launch





Simulate edge cases, validate behavior, and deploy with confidence once quality and compliance checks pass.

- ### Monitor and improve





Track outcomes in production and continuously refine policies using performance and conversation insights.

## Smart Insights

Your agent will surface patterns and uncover root causes, then offer tips on how to update your policies to improve support performance, based on the success metrics you choose.

[Explore Smart Insights](https://giga.ai/insights)

- ### Choose an objective





Select a metric like resolution rate, escalation rate, or customer satisfaction.

- ### Generate insights





AI surfaces prioritized recommendations with projected impact.

- ### Validate at scale





Review transcripts, run hypotheses across thousands of calls, and confirm the root cause.
